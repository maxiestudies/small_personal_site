Maximiliano Estudies

Sound Director | Audio Programmer

I am an Argentinian sound director and audio programmer, currently living in Cologne, Germany. I hold a BA Degree from the Hochschule für Musik und Tanz Köln and a Masters Degree in Sound Direction from the International Ensemble Modern Academy.

Next to my musical work as sound director and designer, I have worked as a programmer and technologist for different composers and interdisciplinary pieces, collaborating in multimedia projects, installations, theater, film and contemporary dance and circus.

I am the founder and director of OMSLO - Bureau for music technology
